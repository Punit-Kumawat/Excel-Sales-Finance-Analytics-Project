# Excel-Sales-Finance-Analytics-Project


## Overview
This project focuses on Sales and Financial Data Analysis using Microsoft Excel to generate insightful reports. The analysis covers **Customer Performance Report, Division Report, Market Performance vs Target Report, New Products - 2021, P&L Statement by Fiscal Year, P&L Statement by Markets, P&L Statement by Months, Top 5 Countries, Top 10 Products, and Top and Bottom Products**. The reports provide a clear picture of business performance, helping in strategic decision-making.

## Project Objectives
### **Sales Report:**
- **Customer Performance Report** ‚Äì Analyze sales trends over multiple years to track customer behavior and purchasing patterns.
- **Division Report** ‚Äì Assess sales and growth patterns at the division level for performance tracking.
- **Market Performance vs Target Report** ‚Äì Compare actual market performance against sales targets to identify improvement areas and growth opportunities.
- **New Products - 2021** ‚Äì Evaluate the sales performance of newly launched products in 2021.
- **Top 5 Countries** ‚Äì Identify the top five countries contributing the most to revenue generation.
- **Top 10 Products** ‚Äì Analyze the best-selling products based on sales volume and revenue.
- **Top and Bottom Products** ‚Äì Identify the highest and lowest-performing products to optimize sales strategies.

### **Finance Report:**
- **P&L Statement by Fiscal Year** ‚Äì Analyze annual profit and loss trends to understand financial performance.
- **P&L Statement by Markets** ‚Äì Evaluate profit and loss across different markets to identify profitability variations.
- **P&L Statement by Months** ‚Äì Track monthly profit and loss data to detect seasonal trends.
